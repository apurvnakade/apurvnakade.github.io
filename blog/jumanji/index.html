Jumanji

December 21, 2017
Filed under Movies

I love movies. I watch a lot of movies, I watch movies about movies, I watch documentaries about movies, I read articles about movies. Sadly I no longer have DC++ so I have to find other ways to do this, like actually going to theaters and paying money to watch.

If you think about all the movies that have come out in the recent times they’re either all crap or all very serious. Even the good humor in the movies is now following the Guardians of the Galaxy and Deadpool style irreverent deadpan humor, which is great, no complains here. Look at the latest Thor for example, I cannot believe I’m saying this but it was actually a good movie thanks to the great sense of humor of it’s director (who also plays a character in the movie btw) or may be I’m biased towards any Led Zeppelin fueled fight sequences.

Hence you should see Jumanji, go watch it now. Forget about Star Wars, that’s boring, you are not fifty years old and Star Wars does not define your childhood.

Goo watch Jumanji. I’ve now become a fan of Kevin Hart. There is no sarcasm, no deadpan humor, nothing vulgar, nothing GTA-Hangover style, nobody ever makes fun of anybody else, all the humor is benignly self-deprecating. It’s a heartwarming, wholesome, adventurous, gripping, innocent movie. I’m pretty sure nobody says fuck or shit in the movie even once. And it’s still funny. I know! Who would have thought? There are a few corny moments but never too many. I had forgotten about the existence of good funny innocent movies. Remember Hera Pheri and Munna Bhai? Hands down the best comedy movies I’ve seen till date.

Gooo watch Jumanji in the theaters so that they make more movies like this.
