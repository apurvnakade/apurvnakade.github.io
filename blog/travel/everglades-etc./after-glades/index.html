After Glades
Filed under Everglades etc., January 6, 2018.

In conclusion I’ve spent 2 days at the Everglades, 1 day at the Biscayne National Park, 1 day at the Miami beach and 1 day mostly driving. Because of the cold coming from the storm I could not do many of the standard things but on the bright side there were no mosquitoes. I could have done a few things better but most things were out of my control, again thanks to the weather. The two things I wish I had done were snorkeling and camping, next time.

To my future self

  1. National Parks are unique and absolutely amazzing, visit as many as you can while you are in the US no matter how much effort and money it takes. Cities are boring.

  2. Look for ranger programs on the National Park websites. Rangers are very knowledgeable people who truly love the wilderness, there cannot be any better guides.

  3. Plan only half day’s worth of trips. Things go wrong all the time and it’s useless to overplan. Also you need the second half of the day to prepare for the next day.

  4. Longer trips are tiring to do alone, because of all the planning, and all the things that can go wrong. When backpacking alone plan smaller 2-3 day trips.

  5. Airbnb’s usually do not have laundry facilities which make things very dirty and uncomfortable after a few days. Airbnb’s are great for small trips but for longer one’s pick a hotel instead.

  6. Fill up your car tank every evening after you’re done with the day, that way you have less to worry about when you’re actually having fun.

  7. Buy groceries at your destination instead of bringing them along with you. Bananas are the best, so is milk, followed by Granny Smiths, and so are granola bars. The optimal thing is to eat exactly 1 heavy meal and survive on fruits for the rest of the day.

  8. Do not worry about money while backpacking, you’ll worry endlessly without actually figuring anything out. Analyze your expenses post-trip or pre-trip.

  9. On a long trip do not plan anything for the last day. You’ll likely be very tired and anxious to get back. It’s best to get an afternoon flight and get back home in the evening so that you can get a good night’s sleep upon returning and be ready to work the next day.

  10. Fuck all advice.
